"Empowering Youth, Leading Scientific Discovery"

What is MYRO?

Malaysian Youth Research Organisation, abbreviated as MYRO, is a Malaysian-based, non-profit, and student-led organisation dedicated to bridging the gap in scientific research exposure for Malaysian secondary school students.

Our Mission

MYRO aims to address the gap in scientific research exposure among Malaysian secondary school students by providing various programs and research opportunities, particularly for underprivileged students.

Research & Learning

We provide access to research opportunities, and workshops, enabling students to explore their scientific curiosity beyond their typical textbook knowledge.

Mentorship

Guiding students through research with expert mentors from various scientific fields.

Annual Symposium

An annual platform for young researchers to present their findings, network with professionals, and gain insights from experts in various scientific fields.
