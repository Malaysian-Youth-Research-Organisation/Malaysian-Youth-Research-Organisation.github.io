About Us

The Malaysian Youth Research Organisation (MYRO) was founded with the aim of empowering young minds across the nation. We believe that the youth are the catalyst for change, and our platform provides opportunities to engage in innovative research, develop skills, and collaborate on solutions to real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To create a future where the youth are fully equipped to drive sustainable development and innovation in Malaysia.

Our Values

  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Inclusivity
  • Integrity

Our Story

Founded in 2020, MYRO has rapidly grown into a leading platform where young innovators and researchers come together to share ideas and build projects that make a real difference. We are committed to fostering a culture of inquiry and creativity, bridging the gap between academic research and practical solutions.
